My Cats

Roksha

Roksha is my first Cat I ever start taking care of myself. He is a great bub. He meows purrs and loves. He does get a bit scared of loud noises and unfamiliar voices though. But when he is comfortable he can be your best bud. He is also great to hug on those rainy days. So he makes for a great support animal. Needless to say I'm very happy I selected him from my local shelter. I was so very close to not choosing him too.

Onix

Onix is my 2nd cat and also my newest one technically. He is a bit smaller than Roksha and younger but has a bunch of energy. When I got Onix I was looking for a broher for Roksha to be his buddy but I was worried they wouldn't get along. Though, my fears were quickly squashed when I saw them both getting along just fine. They've been buddies since day one. And Onix is always purring no matter how you pick him up.
